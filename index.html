Biography

Welcome to the homepage of Nir Lotan, a Machine Learning Researcher and PhD candidate at the University of Haifa. His research bridges the fields of NLP, Computational Social Science, and machine learning with a focus on integrating social information into cutting-edge NLP models.

Nir has led multiple teams in engineering, product management, and data science, while deploying over 30 ML/DL projects. He has a strong foundation in hardware, firmware, and software engineering, complemented by several distinguished invention awards and patents pending.
